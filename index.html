Welcome

A Haven of Comfort & Elegance

Nestled in the heart of Effurun, Jubilee Retreat and Conference Centre offers a harmonious blend of modern luxury and warm hospitality. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our beautifully appointed spaces and attentive service ensure every moment is memorable.
