Our Story

Our Story

A Legacy of Warmth & Distinction

Nestled in the heart of Effurun, Jubilee Retreat and Conference Centre was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where modern luxury meets the soulful warmth of Nigerian hospitality. Every corner of our estate tells a story — of heritage, of thoughtful design, and of an unwavering commitment to excellence.

From the moment you step through our doors, you are welcomed into a space where tradition and contemporary elegance converge. Our beautifully appointed interiors, lush surroundings, and meticulous attention to detail reflect the rich cultural tapestry of the Niger Delta while offering world-class comfort.

— Where every guest becomes family.
